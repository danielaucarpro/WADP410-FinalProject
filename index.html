Daniel Aucar Felipe

UI/UX Designer, Level Designer & Game Designer

My name is Daniel and I am currently based in Vancouver, BC. I am a innovative UI/UX designer and passionate about design in general.

With five years experience as a professional designer, I worked in projects with differents approaches, from graphic design, websites to UI/UX designer for The Path of Calydra game. Throughout these years I acquired the skills and knowledge necessary to start working in the game industry. My goal is to keep learning while I enjoy every step of the design pipeline.

UI/UX Design

My skills as a UI/UX designer include brainstorming, wireframing, prototype, mockup, tablesheet, data analysis, iterate feedback.

Sofwares: Cacoo, Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator, In Vision.

Level Design

My skills include 2D layout, level design document, white boxing, level pipeline, set dressing, iterate feedback. As a level designer I like to implement all my UI/UX knowledge as well.

Sofwares: Unreal, Unity, Maya, Substance.

Graphic Design

My skills include finding references, UI art, logos, web development. With my experience as a graphic designer I created a tool kit set of fundamentals which help to be creative.

Sofwares: Photoshop, Illustrator.
