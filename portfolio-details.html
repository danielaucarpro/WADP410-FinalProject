The Path of Calydra

  • Role: UI/UX Designer, UI Artist
  • Production: 2017 - Now
  • Release: Soon
  • Engine: Unreal 4
  • Link: Calydra.com

The Path of Calydra is a 3D, third person, adventure, puzzle game for PC. Where Matheus is taken to a strange world by a misterious entity known as Calydra. But the only way to survive and get out of this world is to help her to find all powers and forms she once had, and each one of them will help Matheus to overcome all challenges and puzzles in an unique way.
